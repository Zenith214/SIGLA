## 🎯 Slide 2: What is PULSE?

### PULSE Acronym

**P** - **Public**  
**U** - **Understanding**  
**L** - **and**  
**S** - **Local**  
**E** - **Service**  
**E** - **Evaluation**

### System Definition

**PULSE** is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to help **Municipal Local Governance Resource Centers (MLGRC)** systematically:

- 📊 **Collect citizen feedback** on local government services through structured surveys
- 📈 **Analyze service delivery effectiveness** using ML-powered analytics
- 🏆 **Evaluate barangay-level governance performance** with data-driven insights
- 📋 **Generate actionable reports** to support evidence-based decision-making

### Connection to Government Systems

**PULSE integrates with:**
- 🏛️ **DILG (Department of the Interior and Local Government)** - Aligns with national governance standards and SGLGB (Seal of Good Local Governance for Barangays) evaluation criteria
- 🗺️ **Municipal LGU Systems** - Provides barangay-level data for local planning and resource allocation
- 📊 **MLGRC Operations** - Supports Municipal Local Governance Resource Centers in their mandate to improve local governance

### Primary Use Cases

1. **Survey Data Collection** - Interviewers conduct household surveys using digital forms with offline capability
2. **Performance Monitoring** - Administrators track barangay satisfaction scores and service delivery metrics
3. **Strategic Planning** - LGU officials use analytics to identify priority areas for improvement
4. **SGLGB Evaluation** - Support documentation for Seal of Good Local Governance applications
5. **Citizen Priority Action Planning (CPAP)** - Generate data-driven action plans based on citizen feedback
