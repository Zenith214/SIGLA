## 👋 Welcome Officer

As a Barangay Officer, you are responsible for creating and implementing the Citizen Priority Action Plan (CPAP) for your barangay. You use survey results from your community to develop data-driven action plans that improve local governance and service delivery.

### What You Can Do

✅ View your barangay's survey results
✅ Access your barangay's report card
✅ Create and edit CPAP
✅ Use AI suggestions for action items
✅ Submit CPAP for review
✅ Track implementation progress
✅ Communicate with reviewers via comments
✅ View dashboard with auto-selected barangay
✅ Switch between Map and List views
✅ Use hover preview and interactive map

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Login

1. Navigate to the PULSE system URL
2. Enter your credentials
3. Dashboard loads with **your barangay automatically selected**
4. See your barangay's data immediately

### NEW: Auto-Select Feature

**What happens when you login**:
1. System identifies your designated barangay
2. Automatically selects and locks your barangay
3. Fetches historical data
4. Displays your barangay's details in the side panels

**Benefits**:
- No need to search for your barangay
- Instant access to your data
- Saves time on every login
- Reduces confusion

**Note**: You can still explore other barangays by clicking on them or searching.

### Navigation Menu

- **Dashboard**: Main overview (starts with List View by default)
- **Analytics**: Detailed reports for your barangay
- **CPAP**: Create and manage your action plan
- **Profile**: Your account settings

## 🗺️ Dashboard Features

### Default View: List View

**Why List View?**
- Easier to navigate on any device
- Mobile-friendly interface
- Quick access to barangay information
- Simplified data presentation

**What you see**:
- Scrollable list of all barangays
- Search functionality
- Your barangay highlighted (if awardee)
- Click any barangay to view details

### Switching to Map View

**How to switch**:
1. Look for toggle buttons in the **Barangay Details Card header** (upper right)
2. Click **"Map"** to see the geographic visualization
3. Click **"List"** to return to list view

**When to use Map View**:
- Geographic context needed
- Visual exploration
- Hover preview features
- Spatial analysis

**When to use List View**:
- Quick browsing
- Searching by name
- Mobile device
- Simplified interface

### Interactive Map Features (Map View)

**Hover Preview**:
1. Move mouse over any barangay
2. See instant preview of data
3. Blue ring appears around barangay
4. Side panels update automatically

**Click to Lock**:
1. Click on a barangay
2. Selection locks with pin marker
3. Click "View Details" for full modal
4. Access comprehensive information

### Color Coding

Barangays are color-coded by satisfaction level:
- 🟢 **Green**: High satisfaction (80-100)
- 🟡 **Yellow**: Moderate satisfaction (60-79)
- 🟠 **Orange**: Low satisfaction (40-59)
- 🔴 **Red**: Very low satisfaction (0-39)

## 📊 Understanding Your Report Card

### When is it Available?

Your report card is generated after:
- Survey target is met
- Data is processed and analyzed
- Admin activates the report
- You receive a notification

### Accessing Your Report Card

**Steps**:
1. Navigate to **Dashboard**
2. Your barangay is already selected
3. Click **"View Report Card"** or **"View Details"**
4. Report card modal opens

### Report Card Sections

**1. Overall Satisfaction Index**
- Scale: 0-100
- Shows overall community satisfaction
- Color-coded indicator
- Comparison with cutoff (58%)

**Interpretation**:
- **80-100**: Excellent - Community is very satisfied
- **60-79**: Good - Generally positive feedback
- **58-59**: Passing - Meets minimum threshold
- **40-57**: Fair - Needs improvement
- **0-39**: Poor - Significant issues to address

**2. Service Area Ratings**

Six service areas evaluated:
1. **Financial Administration**: Budget transparency, financial management
2. **Disaster Preparedness**: Emergency response, risk reduction
3. **Social Protection**: Health, education, social services
4. **Safety and Peace**: Crime prevention, conflict resolution
5. **Business-Friendly**: Permits, business support
6. **Environmental Management**: Waste management, environmental protection

Each area shows:
- Satisfaction percentage
- Number of respondents
- Top issues mentioned
- Priority level

**3. Action Grid**

Categorizes services into four quadrants:
- **MAINTAIN** (Green): High satisfaction, low need for action
- **OPPORTUNITIES** (Blue): High satisfaction, high need for action
- **MONITOR** (Yellow): Low satisfaction, low need for action
- **FIX NOW** (Red): Low satisfaction, high need for action

**4. Priority Issues**
- Top concerns raised by citizens
- Frequency of mentions
- Severity ratings
- Suggested focus areas

**5. Demographics**
- Age distribution
- Gender breakdown
- Geographic patterns
- Household characteristics

## 📝 Creating Your CPAP

### What is CPAP?

**Citizen Priority Action Plan (CPAP)** is a structured document that:
- Addresses issues identified in survey results
- Defines specific actions to improve services
- Sets timelines and responsibilities
- Tracks implementation progress

### When Can You Start?

**Eligibility**:
- ✅ Survey target is met
- ✅ Report card is generated
- ✅ You're assigned as officer for the barangay
- ✅ Barangay is awarded to active cycle

### Starting Your CPAP

**Steps**:
1. Navigate to **CPAP** → **CPAP Submission**
2. If no CPAP exists, click **"Create a Plan"**
3. System redirects to **Spreadsheet Editor**
4. Begin creating action items

### CPAP Spreadsheet Structure

**13 Columns**:
1. **Results/Observations** - Survey findings
2. **Plan of Action** - Proposed solution
3. **Activity** - Specific activity
4. **Output** - Expected deliverable (REQUIRED)
5. **Actual Output** - What was delivered
6. **Status of Accomplishment** - Current status
7. **Implementation Schedule** - Timeline
8. **Actual Date** - When completed
9. **Financial Requirements** - Budget needed
10. **Responsible Person/Office** - Who is accountable
11. **Committed/To be Committed** - Budget status
12. **Means of Verification** - How to verify
13. **Actions** - Delete row button

**6 Service Areas**:
1. Financial Administration
2. Disaster Preparedness
3. Social Protection
4. Safety and Peace
5. Business-Friendly
6. Environmental Management

### Creating Action Items

**Step-by-Step**:

1. **Select Service Area**
   - Choose area needing improvement
   - Based on your report card

2. **Click "Add row for [Service Area]"**
   - Opens new row in spreadsheet

3. **Fill in Required Fields**:

**Results/Observations**:
```
Example: "Low satisfaction with health services (45%). 
Citizens report long wait times at health center and 
lack of available medicines."
```

**Plan of Action**:
```
Example: "Improve health center efficiency and ensure 
adequate medicine supply"
```

**Activity**:
```
Example: "1) Implement appointment scheduling system
2) Hire additional health worker
3) Establish medicine inventory system"
```

**Output** (REQUIRED):
```
Example: "Functional appointment system, 1 new health 
worker hired, medicine inventory system operational"
```

**Implementation Schedule**:
```
Example: "January - June 2026"
```

**Financial Requirements**:
```
Example: "₱200,000.00
- Appointment system: ₱50,000
- Health worker salary: ₱120,000
- Inventory system: ₱30,000"
```

**Responsible Person/Office**:
```
Example: "Barangay Health Officer / Health Committee"
```

**Means of Verification**:
```
Example: "Appointment logs, employment contract, 
inventory records, patient satisfaction survey"
```

4. **Add More Rows** as needed (3-5 per service area recommended)

5. **Save Your Work** - Click "Save Plan" or "Save All Changes"

### Tips for Good Action Items

✅ **DO**:
- Address specific issues from survey
- Set realistic timelines
- Assign clear responsibilities
- Define measurable outputs
- Justify budget requests
- Include verification methods

❌ **DON'T**:
- Be vague or general
- Set unrealistic goals
- Leave fields blank
- Ignore survey findings
- Overestimate budget
- Forget to save

## 🤖 Using AI Suggestions

### What is AI Suggestions?

AI analyzes your survey results and generates recommended action items automatically, saving you time and providing data-driven suggestions.

### How to Use AI Suggestions

**Steps**:
1. Click **"Get AI Suggestions"** button (above spreadsheet)
2. System analyzes your survey data
3. Modal shows how many suggestions were created
4. Click **"OK"**
5. AI-generated rows appear with **purple background**
6. Warning banner appears at top

### Understanding AI Suggestions

**What AI Provides**:
- Service area identification
- Observation based on survey data
- Suggested plan of action
- Recommended output
- Estimated timeline
- Suggested verification methods

**What AI Does NOT Provide**:
- Specific budget amounts (you must add)
- Responsible person names (you must assign)
- Local context (you must customize)
- Final decision (you have full control)

### Editing AI Suggestions

**How to Edit**:
1. Click on any cell in purple row
2. Modify the text
3. Add missing information
4. Customize for local context
5. Save changes

**What to Customize**:
- Budget amounts (AI doesn't know local costs)
- Responsible persons (assign specific names)
- Timeline (adjust for local capacity)
- Activities (add specific local actions)
- Verification methods (add local procedures)

### Managing AI Suggestions

**Accept Suggestions**:
- Edit as needed
- Fill in missing fields
- Save the plan

**Reject Suggestions**:
- Click trash icon on individual rows
- Or click "Clear All Suggestions" to remove all

**Mix and Match**:
- Keep some AI suggestions
- Delete others
- Add your own manual rows
- Create hybrid plan

### Important Warnings

⚠️ **AI Limitations**:
- May contain inaccuracies
- Doesn't know local context
- Budget estimates may be off
- Requires human review
- Not a replacement for your judgment

✅ **Your Responsibility**:
- Review every suggestion
- Verify accuracy
- Customize for local needs
- Add missing details
- Make final decisions

## 📤 Submitting for Review

### Before Submitting

**Checklist**:
- [ ] All required fields filled (especially Output)
- [ ] At least 1 action item per priority service area
- [ ] Budget estimates are reasonable
- [ ] Timelines are realistic
- [ ] Responsible persons are confirmed
- [ ] Means of verification are clear
- [ ] Reviewed for accuracy
- [ ] Saved all changes

### Submission Process

**Steps**:
1. Click **"Submit for Review"** button (top right)
2. Review submission confirmation dialog
3. Click **"Confirm Submission"**
4. Status changes to **"SUBMITTED"**
5. CPAP is locked (no more edits)
6. Admin/Reviewer is notified

### What Happens Next?

**Admin Reviews Your CPAP**:
1. Admin receives notification
2. Reviews your action items
3. May add comments or questions
4. Makes a decision

**Possible Outcomes**:

**1. Approved** ✅
- You receive notification
- Can start implementation
- Track progress in spreadsheet

**2. Revision Requested** 🔄
- You receive notification with feedback
- CPAP is unlocked
- Make requested changes
- Resubmit for review

**3. Rejected** ❌ (rare)
- You receive notification with reasons
- Must start over
- Contact admin for guidance

### Using Comments

**Purpose**: Communicate with admin/reviewer

**How to Use**:
1. Click **"Comments"** button (right side)
2. Sidebar opens
3. Type your message
4. Press **Enter** or click **Send**

**When to Comment**:
- Ask questions about requirements
- Explain challenges or delays
- Provide additional context
- Respond to admin feedback
- Share updates

**Example Comments**:
```
"We're having difficulty finding a contractor for 
Item 3. Can we extend the timeline by one month?"

"Item 5 budget was estimated based on quotes from 
three local suppliers. Happy to provide documentation."

"Thank you for the feedback! I've updated Items 2 and 4 
as requested. Ready for re-review."
```

## 📈 Tracking Implementation

### After Approval

Once your CPAP is approved, you can track implementation progress:

**Steps**:
1. Navigate to **CPAP** → **CPAP Submission**
2. Click **"Edit in Spreadsheet View"**
3. Update these columns:
   - **Actual Output**: What was actually delivered
   - **Status of Accomplishment**: Current status
   - **Actual Date**: When completed
4. Click **"Save All Changes"**

### Status Options

- **Not Started**: No work has begun
- **In Progress**: Currently working on it
- **Completed**: Finished successfully
- **Delayed**: Behind schedule, needs attention

### Progress Tracking

**System Calculates**:
- Overall completion percentage
- Status per service area
- Timeline adherence
- Budget utilization

**Admin Sees**:
- Your progress updates
- Completion rates
- Delayed items
- Implementation trends

### Regular Updates

**Best Practice**:
- Update status monthly
- Document actual outputs
- Note completion dates
- Explain delays
- Celebrate successes

## ✅ Best Practices

### Creating Effective CPAPs

1. **Start with Survey Data**: Base actions on actual community feedback
2. **Be Specific**: Clear, measurable outputs
3. **Be Realistic**: Achievable timelines and budgets
4. **Assign Clearly**: Specific persons, not just offices
5. **Plan Verification**: How will you prove completion?

### Using AI Wisely

1. **Use as Starting Point**: Don't accept blindly
2. **Customize Everything**: Add local context
3. **Verify Accuracy**: Check against survey data
4. **Fill Gaps**: Add budget, persons, details
5. **Mix with Manual**: Combine AI and your ideas

### Communication Tips

1. **Be Proactive**: Ask questions early
2. **Be Responsive**: Reply to admin comments promptly
3. **Be Clear**: Explain your reasoning
4. **Be Professional**: Maintain respectful tone
5. **Be Honest**: Admit challenges, ask for help

### Implementation Success

1. **Start Early**: Don't wait until deadline
2. **Monitor Regularly**: Check progress weekly
3. **Document Everything**: Keep records
4. **Communicate Issues**: Report problems early
5. **Update System**: Keep CPAP current

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Issue: Can't See CPAP Page**
- Verify you have Officer role
- Check barangay assignment
- Confirm survey target is met
- Ensure report card is generated
- Contact admin

**Issue: Can't Submit CPAP**
- Check all required fields (especially Output)
- Ensure at least one action item exists
- Verify you clicked "Save" first
- Try refreshing page
- Contact admin if persists

**Issue: AI Suggestions Not Working**
- Ensure survey data exists
- Check report card is generated
- Refresh page and try again
- Clear browser cache
- Contact admin if ML service is down

**Issue: Auto-Select Not Working**
- Verify you have barangay designation
- Check barangay exists in system
- Refresh the page
- Contact admin to verify assignment

**Issue: View Toggle Not Showing**
- Look in Barangay Details Card header (upper right)
- Ensure you're on dashboard page
- Verify you have Officer role
- Try refreshing page
