## 👋 Welcome Interviewer

As an Interviewer, you are the frontline data collector for the PULSE system. You conduct face-to-face surveys with community members, ensuring accurate and complete data collection that will help improve local governance.

### What You Can Do

✅ View your assigned spots
✅ Conduct surveys with respondents
✅ Use Kish Grid for respondent selection
✅ Submit completed surveys
✅ Save drafts for incomplete surveys
✅ Work offline when internet is unavailable
✅ View your progress and quotas

**Important**: Your role is focused on data collection. You will NOT see the dashboard that other roles access.

---

## 🎯 Your Responsibilities

### Primary Tasks

1. **Survey Conduct**
   - Interview selected respondents
   - Ask questions clearly and accurately
   - Record responses immediately
   - Ensure complete submissions

2. **Respondent Selection**
   - Use Kish Grid method correctly
   - Interview only the selected person
   - Follow random selection rules
   - No substitutions allowed

3. **Data Quality**
   - Complete all required fields
   - Verify responses before submitting
   - Ensure GPS accuracy (if enabled)
   - Maintain respondent privacy

4. **Quota Management**
   - Meet assigned quotas
   - Work within designated spots
   - Submit surveys on time
   - Report issues promptly

5. **Professional Conduct**
   - Be respectful and courteous
   - Maintain confidentiality
   - Follow ethical guidelines
   - Represent the system professionally

## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Login

1. Navigate to the PULSE system URL
2. Enter your credentials
3. You'll see your **Survey Interface** (not the dashboard)

### Your Interface

**Main Sections**:
- **My Assignments**: List of assigned spots
- **Start Survey**: Begin a new survey
- **Drafts**: Resume incomplete surveys
- **My Progress**: View your completion status
- **Profile**: Your account settings

### Understanding Your Assignment

**View Your Spots**:
1. Login to the system
2. See list of **assigned spots**
3. Each spot shows:
   - Spot name and location
   - Your quota
   - Progress so far
   - Remaining surveys needed
   - Deadline (if set)

**Example Assignment**:
```
Spot: Market Area, San Jose
Location: Public Market, Stall Section
Quota: 25 surveys
Completed: 10
Remaining: 15
Deadline: January 15, 2026
```

## 📱 Conducting Surveys

### Preparation

**Before Going to the Field**:
- ✅ Charge your device fully
- ✅ Bring backup power bank
- ✅ Review survey questions
- ✅ Know your spot location
- ✅ Bring identification
- ✅ Have contact info for supervisor
- ✅ Test internet connection

**What to Bring**:
- Mobile device or tablet
- Power bank
- ID card
- Pen and paper (backup)
- Spot location map
- Supervisor contact info

### Starting a Survey

**Steps**:
1. Navigate to **Start Survey**
2. Select your **Spot** from dropdown
3. System shows survey form
4. Begin with respondent selection

### Survey Sections

**Section 1: Respondent Selection (Kish Grid)**
- Count household members
- System selects respondent randomly
- Interview ONLY the selected person

**Section 2: Basic Information**
- Name (optional)
- Age
- Gender
- Contact information
- Address

**Section 3: Service Area Questions**
- Financial Administration
- Disaster Preparedness
- Social Protection
- Safety and Peace
- Business-Friendly
- Environmental Management

**Section 4: Demographics**
- Household size
- Income level
- Education
- Employment

**Section 5: Additional Feedback**
- Open-ended comments
- Suggestions
- Concerns

**Section 6: Geolocation** (if enabled)
- Allow location access
- System captures GPS coordinates
- Verify accuracy

### Recording Responses

**Rating Scales**:
- **1-5 Scale**: 1 = Very Dissatisfied, 5 = Very Satisfied
- **Yes/No**: Clear binary choices
- **Multiple Choice**: Select one option
- **Text Fields**: Write clearly and completely

**Tips for Accuracy**:
- Read questions exactly as written
- Don't suggest answers
- Record immediately
- Clarify if respondent is unsure
- Use "Not Applicable" when appropriate

### Completing the Survey

**Before Submitting**:
1. Review all sections for completeness
2. Check that all required fields are filled
3. Verify GPS coordinates (if enabled)
4. Confirm respondent information
5. Thank the respondent

**Submit Options**:
- **Submit**: Complete and send immediately
- **Save as Draft**: Save for later completion

## 🎲 Using the Kish Grid

### What is the Kish Grid?

The **Kish Grid** is a statistical method for randomly selecting one person from a household to interview. This ensures unbiased, representative data.

### How It Works

**Step 1: Count Household Members**
- Count all people living in the household
- Include all ages (adults and children)
- Don't count visitors or temporary guests

**Step 2: System Selects Respondent**
- Enter the number of household members
- System uses Kish Grid algorithm
- System tells you which person to interview
- Example: "Interview the 3rd oldest person"

**Step 3: Interview Selected Person**
- Interview ONLY the selected person
- No substitutions allowed
- If person is unavailable, note it and move to next household
- Don't interview someone else from the same household

### Important Rules

✅ **DO**:
- Count accurately
- Follow the selection exactly
- Interview only the selected person
- Return later if person is unavailable

❌ **DON'T**:
- Substitute with another household member
- Interview whoever is convenient
- Skip the Kish Grid process
- Interview multiple people from same household

### Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1**:
```
Household: 5 members
System selects: 2nd oldest person
Action: Interview the 2nd oldest person in the household
```

**Scenario 2**:
```
Household: 3 members
System selects: Youngest person
Selected person: Not home
Action: Thank them and move to next household
```

## 📴 Offline Mode

### What is Offline Mode?

When internet is unavailable, the system automatically switches to **offline mode**, allowing you to continue conducting surveys. Responses are saved locally and sync when internet returns.

### How It Works

**Automatic Activation**:
1. System detects no internet connection
2. Switches to offline mode automatically
3. You see an "Offline" indicator
4. Continue conducting surveys normally

**Data Storage**:
- Surveys are saved on your device
- Stored securely in browser
- No data is lost
- Syncs automatically when online

**Syncing**:
1. When internet returns, system detects it
2. Saved surveys upload automatically
3. You see sync progress
4. Confirmation when complete

### Best Practices for Offline Mode

**Before Going Offline**:
- ✅ Ensure device is fully charged
- ✅ Test offline mode beforehand
- ✅ Know how many surveys you'll conduct
- ✅ Have backup plan for technical issues

**While Offline**:
- ✅ Continue surveys as normal
- ✅ Monitor device battery
- ✅ Don't clear browser data
- ✅ Keep device secure

**After Returning Online**:
- ✅ Connect to internet
- ✅ Wait for sync to complete
- ✅ Verify all surveys uploaded
- ✅ Check for confirmation messages

### Important Warnings

⚠️ **DON'T**:
- Clear browser cache while offline
- Close browser before syncing
- Use multiple devices for same spot
- Uninstall app before syncing

## 📝 Managing Drafts

### Saving Drafts

**When to Save as Draft**:
- Respondent needs to pause
- You need to take a break
- Survey is interrupted
- Need to verify information

**How to Save**:
1. Click **"Save as Draft"** button
2. Draft is saved on your device
3. You can close the survey
4. Resume anytime

### Resuming Drafts

**Steps**:
1. Go to **Drafts** section
2. See list of saved drafts with:
   - Respondent name (if entered)
   - Date saved
   - Completion percentage
   - Spot name
3. Click on a draft to resume
4. Continue from where you left off
5. Complete and submit

### Managing Multiple Drafts

**Best Practices**:
- Don't accumulate too many drafts
- Complete drafts within 24 hours
- Delete drafts you won't complete
- Keep drafts organized

**Deleting Drafts**:
1. Go to **Drafts** section
2. Find the draft
3. Click **"Delete"**
4. Confirm deletion

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Issue: Can't Submit Survey**
- Check internet connection
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Try saving as draft first
- Contact supervisor if issue persists

**Issue: GPS Not Working**
- Enable location services on device
- Allow browser to access location
- Go outside for better signal
- Try refreshing the page

**Issue: Offline Mode Not Working**
- Use supported browser (Chrome, Firefox)
- Allow storage permissions
- Don't clear browser data
- Test before going to field

**Issue: Draft Not Saving**
- Check device storage space
- Ensure browser allows local storage
- Try different browser
- Contact supervisor

**Issue: Can't See My Assignments**
- Verify you're logged in
- Check if you have Interviewer role
- Confirm supervisor assigned you
- Contact supervisor
