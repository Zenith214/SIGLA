## Getting Started

### Accessing the CPAP Module

1. Log in to the PULSE system with your OFFICER credentials
2. Click on your profile menu in the top-right corner
3. Select **"CPAP Submission"** from the dropdown menu
4. You'll be taken to your barangay's CPAP editor

### Understanding CPAP Status

Your CPAP can be in one of four states:

- **Draft**: You can freely edit all fields and items
- **Submitted**: Awaiting DILG review (read-only for you)
- **Revision Requested**: DILG has requested changes (editable)
- **Approved**: Approved by DILG (only progress fields editable)

## Creating a CPAP

### Step 1: Access Your CPAP

When you first access the CPAP module, a new CPAP will be automatically created for your barangay and the current survey cycle in **Draft** status.

### Step 2: Add Action Items

Each CPAP consists of multiple action items. To add an item:

1. Click the **"Add New Item"** button
2. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Priority Area**: The service area or issue being addressed (e.g., "Health Services", "Water Supply")
   - **Target Output**: What you plan to accomplish (e.g., "Establish mobile health clinic")
   - **Success Indicator**: How you'll measure success (e.g., "50 residents served per month")
   - **Responsible Person**: Who will lead this initiative (e.g., "Barangay Health Worker")
   - **Timeline Start**: When you'll begin implementation
   - **Timeline End**: Target completion date
3. Click **"Save Item"**

### Step 3: Edit or Delete Items

While in **Draft** status:

- Click the **pencil icon** to edit an existing item
- Click the **trash icon** to delete an item
- Changes are saved automatically

### Best Practices

✅ **DO:**
- Be specific and measurable in your target outputs
- Set realistic timelines
- Align priority areas with survey feedback
- Include multiple action items for comprehensive coverage

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use vague language like "improve services"
- Set unrealistic deadlines
- Leave required fields empty
- Submit without reviewing all items

## Using AI Suggestions

The CPAP module includes an AI-powered feature that analyzes your barangay's survey results and generates action recommendations.

### How to Use AI Suggestions

1. While your CPAP is in **Draft** status, click the **"AI Suggestions"** button
2. The system will analyze survey data and generate recommendations
3. A modal will appear showing suggestions grouped by timeline:
   - **Short-term** (0-3 months)
   - **Medium-term** (6-12 months)
   - **Long-term** (1+ years)

### Reviewing AI Suggestions

Each suggestion includes:
- Priority area (based on survey feedback)
- Recommended target output
- Suggested success indicator
- Timeline recommendation
- Source (which service area it came from)

### Using Suggestions

You have three options:

1. **Use All Suggestions**: Click **"Use These Suggestions"** to pre-fill your CPAP with all AI-generated items
2. **Manual Selection**: Review suggestions and manually create items based on selected recommendations
3. **Ignore Suggestions**: Close the modal and create items manually

### Important Notes

⚠️ **AI Suggestions are Optional**
- You are NOT required to use AI suggestions
- You have full control over your final CPAP content
- AI-generated items are NOT automatically saved

⚠️ **Review and Edit**
- Always review AI-generated items before saving
- Edit items to match your local context
- Add responsible persons and adjust timelines
- Delete suggestions that don't fit your needs

### Example Workflow

```
1. Click "AI Suggestions" button
2. Review the generated recommendations
3. Click "Use These Suggestions"
4. Edit each item to add:
   - Specific responsible person names
   - Adjusted timelines based on local capacity
   - Context-specific details
5. Delete any items that aren't relevant
6. Add additional manual items as needed
7. Save your CPAP
```

## Submitting for Review

### Before You Submit

Ensure all items have:
- ✅ Complete priority area
- ✅ Specific target output
- ✅ Measurable success indicator
- ✅ Named responsible person
- ✅ Valid timeline dates (start before end)

### Submission Process

1. Review all your action items carefully
2. Click the **"Submit to DILG for Review"** button
3. Confirm your submission in the dialog
4. Your CPAP status will change to **Submitted**

### What Happens Next

- DILG administrators will be notified of your submission
- Your CPAP becomes read-only while under review
- You'll receive a notification when DILG makes a decision
- DILG can either **Approve** or **Request Revision**

### Validation Errors

If submission fails, you'll see specific error messages:

- **"At least one item is required"**: Add action items before submitting
- **"Item X is missing required fields"**: Complete all fields for that item
- **"Timeline end must be after start"**: Fix date ranges

## Handling Revision Requests

### When DILG Requests Revisions

If DILG requests changes:

1. You'll receive a notification with their comments
2. Your CPAP status changes to **Revision Requested**
3. All fields become editable again
4. DILG comments are displayed prominently at the top

### Making Revisions

1. Read the DILG comments carefully
2. Make the requested changes to your action items
3. You can:
   - Edit existing items
   - Add new items
   - Delete items
   - Completely restructure your plan
4. Click **"Resubmit to DILG"** when ready

### Resubmission

- The resubmission process is the same as initial submission
- Your CPAP returns to **Submitted** status
- DILG will review your revised plan

## Tracking Progress

### After Approval

Once DILG approves your CPAP:

1. Your CPAP status changes to **Approved**
2. Most fields become read-only
3. Progress tracking fields become editable

### Updating Progress

For each action item, you can update:

- **Actual Output**: What you've actually accomplished
- **Accomplishment Status**: Current status (e.g., "In Progress", "Completed", "Delayed")
- **Remarks**: Additional notes or explanations

### How to Update Progress

1. Navigate to your approved CPAP
2. Find the item you want to update
3. Fill in the progress fields
4. Click **"Save Progress Update"**
5. The update timestamp is recorded for DILG visibility

### Progress Update Best Practices

✅ **DO:**
- Update progress regularly (monthly recommended)
- Be honest about challenges and delays
- Provide specific accomplishment details
- Document lessons learned in remarks

❌ **DON'T:**
- Wait until the end to update progress
- Exaggerate accomplishments
- Leave remarks empty when there are issues
- Ignore items that are behind schedule

### Example Progress Updates

**Good Example:**
```
Actual Output: "Mobile health clinic established, serving 45 residents per month"
Accomplishment Status: "On Track"
Remarks: "Exceeded initial target by 10%. Planning to extend hours."
```

**Poor Example:**
```
Actual Output: "Done"
Accomplishment Status: "Complete"
Remarks: ""
```

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "I can't find the CPAP Submission button"

**Solution:**
- Verify you're logged in as an OFFICER user
- Check with your administrator if your role is correct
- Try logging out and back in

#### "I can't edit my CPAP"

**Possible Causes:**
- Your CPAP is in **Submitted** status (wait for DILG review)
- Your CPAP is **Approved** (only progress fields are editable)

**Solution:**
- Check the status badge at the top of the page
- If submitted, wait for DILG response
- If approved, use the progress tracking fields only

#### "AI Suggestions button doesn't work"

**Solution:**
- Ensure your CPAP is in **Draft** status
- Check that survey data exists for your barangay and cycle
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if the issue persists

#### "Submission fails with validation errors"

**Solution:**
- Read the error messages carefully
- Check each item for missing required fields
- Ensure timeline dates are valid (start before end)
- Verify you have at least one complete item

#### "Changes aren't saving"

**Solution:**
- Check your internet connection
- Look for error messages at the top of the page
- Try refreshing the page (unsaved changes may be lost)
- Contact support if the issue persists

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues not covered in this guide:

1. Check the CPAP Quick Reference Guide
2. Contact your DILG administrator
3. Email technical support with:
   - Your username
   - Barangay name
   - Description of the issue
   - Screenshots if applicable

## Tips for Success

### Writing Effective Action Items

**Priority Area:**
- Use clear, specific service categories
- Align with survey feedback themes
- Example: "Water Supply and Sanitation" not just "Water"

**Target Output:**
- Be specific and actionable
- Include quantities when possible
- Example: "Install 5 new water refilling stations" not "Improve water access"

**Success Indicator:**
- Make it measurable
- Include numbers or percentages
- Example: "80% of residents report improved water access" not "Better water"

**Responsible Person:**
- Name specific individuals or positions
- Ensure they're aware of their responsibility
- Example: "Engr. Juan dela Cruz, Barangay Engineer"

**Timeline:**
- Be realistic about your capacity
- Consider budget cycles and seasons
- Build in buffer time for delays
- Coordinate timelines across related items

### Leveraging Survey Data

Before creating your CPAP:

1. Review your barangay's survey results in the Report Card
2. Identify the lowest-performing service areas
3. Look at specific feedback and complaints
4. Prioritize issues affecting the most residents
5. Consider both quick wins and long-term improvements

### Collaboration Tips

- Consult with barangay council members
- Involve department heads in planning
- Get input from community leaders
- Coordinate with neighboring barangays on shared issues
- Align with municipal development plans

## Quick Reference Card

### CPAP Workflow

```
Draft → Submit → Submitted → Review → Approved → Track Progress
                      ↓
                Revision Requested → Revise → Resubmit
```

### Status-Based Actions

| Status | You Can | You Cannot |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Draft | Edit all fields, add/delete items, submit | Update progress |
| Submitted | View only | Edit, submit |
| Revision Requested | Edit all fields, add/delete items, resubmit | Update progress |
| Approved | Update progress fields | Edit action items |

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Ctrl + S`: Save changes (when editing)
- `Esc`: Close modals
- `Tab`: Navigate between fields
