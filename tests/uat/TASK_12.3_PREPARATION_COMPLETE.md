✅ New workflow doesn't slow down operations  
✅ System works consistently without crashes  
✅ Training materials adequately prepare staff  

### Go/No-Go Decision Criteria

**GO Decision** (Deploy to production):
- All critical issues resolved
- 90%+ success metrics achieved
- Positive feedback from majority of participants
- No deployment blockers remaining

**CONDITIONAL GO** (Deploy with conditions):
- Minor issues remain but have workarounds
- 80%+ success metrics achieved
- Some concerns but manageable
- Specific conditions must be met

**NO-GO Decision** (Delay deployment):
- Critical issues unresolved
- <80% success metrics
- Significant negative feedback
- Major usability problems

## Risk Management

### High-Priority Risks

**Risk 1: Low FI Participation**
- **Mitigation**: Recruit 10-12 FIs for 6-8 target, offer incentives
- **Contingency**: Extend recruitment period, adjust pilot scope

**Risk 2: Technical Issues During Pilot**
- **Mitigation**: Thorough pre-testing, technical support on-site
- **Contingency**: Backup devices, direct line to developers

**Risk 3: Poor GPS Signal**
- **Mitigation**: Test in multiple locations, train on troubleshooting
- **Contingency**: Document limitations, adjust expectations

### Medium-Priority Risks

**Risk 4: Training Inadequate**
- **Mitigation**: Pre-review materials, multiple training methods
- **Contingency**: Additional training sessions, enhanced support

**Risk 5: Negative Feedback**
- **Mitigation**: Emphasize benefits, be open to feedback
- **Contingency**: Adjust features, improve training
