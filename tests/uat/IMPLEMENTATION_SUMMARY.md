### Quantitative Targets

| Metric | Target |

### Go/No-Go Decision

- **GO**: All critical issues resolved, 90%+ metrics, positive feedback
- **CONDITIONAL GO**: Minor issues with workarounds, 80-89% metrics
- **NO-GO**: Critical issues unresolved, <80% metrics, negative feedback
